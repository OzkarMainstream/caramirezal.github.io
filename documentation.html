Data Science BLOG

Facebook-Cambridge Analytica scandal has recently brought to the public attention the current importance of data. At the present, companies are very interested in getting data from their customers in order to look for their consuming patterns, and ultimately increase their sells, and revenues. For the other hand, new medical technologies allow to get ("sequence" is the right term) the whole genetic information of an individual. Individual genetic information can in theory be used to predict its predisposition to develop some diseases and is the essence of the so call personalized medicine. However, this raises an analytical problem. A human genome is similar to a book with 3 billion letters but for statistical reasons finding patterns in these letters requires to sequence not one but hundreds of different genomes which means a lot of data to analyze (see!).
These two examples may illustrate the emerging importance of Data Science. This blog is intended to show implementations, pipelines, and interesting sources concerning data analysis and machine learning algorithms on real data.
